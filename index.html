QUIZ STRUCTURE: Format: 15 questions across 5 categories (3 questions each) Time: ~5 minutes Scoring: 0-3 points per question (60 points max) Results: 4 tiers with specific next steps THE 15 QUESTIONS: CATEGORY 1: IDENTITY & PURPOSE (Questions 1-3) Q1: When you think about retirement, what excites you most? Finally having time to relax and do nothing (0 pts) Getting away from work stress (1 pt) Spending more time on hobbies I enjoy (2 pts) Starting new projects and pursuing meaningful goals (3 pts) Q2: How do you introduce yourself at social gatherings? By my job title or company (0 pts) By what I used to do professionally (1 pt) By a mix of work and personal interests (2 pts) By my interests, values, or what I'm currently working on (3 pts) Q3: If your typical week suddenly had no structure, you would: Feel lost without a schedule (0 pts) Welcome the break but worry after a few weeks (1 pt) Have some ideas but need time to figure it out (2 pts) Know exactly how I'd fill my time meaningfully (3 pts) CATEGORY 2: FINANCIAL READINESS (Questions 4-6) Q4: If the market dropped 30% in your first year of retirement, you would: Panic and consider going back to work (0 pts) Feel very stressed and unsure what to do (1 pt) Be concerned but know I have a plan to adjust (2 pts) Stay calm—my withdrawal strategy accounts for this (3 pts) Q5: How confident are you in your Social Security claiming strategy? I haven't thought about timing at all (0 pts) I know I should wait, but I'm not sure why (1 pt) I've done some research and have a general idea (2 pts) I've run the numbers and know my optimal claiming age (3 pts) Q6: When it comes to spending in retirement: I'm not sure how much I can safely spend (0 pts) I have a rough budget but no withdrawal plan (1 pt) I know my safe withdrawal rate but haven't tested it (2 pts) I have a dynamic withdrawal strategy with guardrails (3 pts) CATEGORY 3: RELATIONSHIPS & HOUSEHOLD (Questions 7-9) Q7: Have you and your spouse discussed what daily life looks like in retirement? No, we haven't had that conversation (0 pts) We've talked casually but made no decisions (1 pt) We've discussed expectations but haven't written anything down (2 pts) We've had structured conversations and aligned on boundaries (3 pts) Q8: How much time do you currently spend with friends outside of work? Almost none—my social life is mostly work-based (0 pts) Occasionally, but it's not consistent (1 pt) Regularly, but I'll need to rebuild after retirement (2 pts) I have strong friendships independent of work (3 pts) Q9: If you and your spouse were home together every day: We'd probably drive each other crazy (0 pts) It would be an adjustment we'd figure out (1 pt) We'd need some ground rules but we'd be fine (2 pts) We've already discussed space, routines, and boundaries (3 pts) CATEGORY 4: HEALTH & LONGEVITY (Questions 10-12) Q10: How would you rate your current physical health? Poor—I've been neglecting it for years (0 pts) Fair—I know I need to make changes (1 pt) Good—I exercise sometimes but not consistently (2 pts) Excellent—I have a consistent fitness routine (3 pts) Q11: When was your last comprehensive health screening? More than 3 years ago (or never) (0 pts) 1-2 years ago (1 pt) Within the past year (2 pts) Within the past 6 months, and I know my key health metrics (3 pts) Q12: How do you challenge your brain regularly? I don't—most of my time is passive consumption (TV, scrolling) (0 pts) Occasionally through work, but not intentionally (1 pt) I read regularly and do some puzzles (2 pts) I'm actively learning new skills or languages (3 pts) CATEGORY 5: LOGISTICS & PLANNING (Questions 13-15) Q13: Do you have updated legal documents (will, POA, healthcare directive)? No, I haven't done any of this (0 pts) I have some outdated documents (1 pt) I have updated documents but haven't communicated them (2 pts) Everything is current and my family knows where to find them (3 pts) Q14: If something happened to you tomorrow, could your spouse access critical accounts? No—they wouldn't know where to start (0 pts) Maybe—some things are documented, others aren't (1 pt) Mostly—we've discussed it but haven't organized everything (2 pts) Yes—we have a complete digital estate plan and command center (3 pts) Q15: How clear are you on what you'll do in the first 90 days of retirement? No idea—I'll figure it out when I get there (0 pts) Some general thoughts but no plan (1 pt) I have ideas but haven't committed to anything specific (2 pts) I have a written 90-day transition plan with concrete actions (3 pts) SCORING & RESULTS: SCORE CALCULATION: Total possible points: 45 (15 questions × 3 points max) TIER 1: READY (38-45 points) Headline: You're Ready—But Stay Sharp Result message: You've done the work most people skip.
